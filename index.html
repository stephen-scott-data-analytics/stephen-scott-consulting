Start with a data health check

A data health check is a practical review of how your business currently captures, stores, reports and uses data. It is designed to quickly identify where better insight could save time, reduce waste or support better decisions.

What you have

A review of your current spreadsheets, reports, systems, data sources and key business metrics.

What is not working

Identification of manual work, duplicated effort, reporting gaps, inconsistent definitions and unreliable outputs.

What to do next

A clear, practical set of recommendations prioritised by business value, effort and impact.

How it works

Simple, practical and focused on finding useful answers quickly.

Review
